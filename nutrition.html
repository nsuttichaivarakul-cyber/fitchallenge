5 Simple Rules — No Calorie Counting Needed
1
Eat protein every meal
Egg, chicken, fish, or tofu at every meal. Most important rule.
2
Half your plate = vegetables
Fill half with veggies first, then protein, then rice or carbs.
3
No sugary drinks
Bubble tea, sweetened iced coffee, soda — all hidden calories. Switch to plain water.
4
Stop eating 3 hours before bed
Your body burns fat during sleep. Don't interrupt it with food.
5
Walk 10,000 steps daily
Burns 300–500 extra calories. More effective than skipping meals.
